# Chain of Thought (CoT) Reasoning

**IMPORTANT: You must use Chain of Thought reasoning for each step.**

For each signaling question, you should:
1. **Read and understand** the question carefully
2. **Search the manuscript** for relevant information that addresses the question
3. **Analyze the evidence** you found:
   - Does it directly answer the question?
   - What are the key criteria for each answer code (Y, PY, PN, N, NI, NA)?
   - Does the evidence meet the criteria for a specific answer?
4. **Reason step by step**:
   - First, identify what information is needed to answer the question
   - Then, locate relevant sentences or paragraphs in the manuscript
   - Next, evaluate whether the evidence matches the criteria for each possible answer
   - Finally, select the most appropriate answer code based on your analysis
5. **Extract verbatim evidence** that supports your answer

For the domain-level risk judgment:
1. **Review all your answers** to the signaling questions in this domain
2. **Consider the RoB 2 guidance** on how to aggregate individual question answers into a domain-level judgment
3. **Reason step by step**:
   - Which questions indicate risk of bias?
   - What is the severity of the concerns identified?
   - How do the answers collectively inform the overall domain risk?
   - Apply the RoB 2 decision rules to determine the final judgment
4. **Justify your judgment** based on the pattern of answers

# Task Rules

1. **Strict Answer Codes**: You must ONLY use the following codes for the `answer_code` field. Do not use full words.
   - **Y** = Yes
   - **PY** = Probably Yes
   - **PN** = Probably No
   - **N** = No
   - **NI** = No Information (Use this if the manuscript does not mention the method)
   - **NA** = Not Applicable
2.  **Verbatim Evidence Requirement**:
   - For the `evidence_quote` field: You must extract the **exact verbatim sentence(s)** from the manuscript that justifies your answer code.
   - **Do not paraphrase.** Copy paste the text exactly.
   - If `answer_code` is **NI** (No Information), the `evidence_quote` must be exactly: "No information provided in text".
3. **Chain of Thought Requirement**:
   - For each signaling question, include a `reasoning` field that shows your step-by-step thinking process
   - Explain what evidence you found, how you evaluated it, and why you chose that specific answer code
   - For the domain-level risk judgment, include reasoning that explains how you aggregated the individual question answers
