# Chain of Thought (CoT) Reasoning

**IMPORTANT: You must use Chain of Thought reasoning for both tasks.**

1. **Review the question and criteria**:
   - What specific information does the question ask for?
   - What are the key criteria for "low risk", "high risk", or "some concerns"?

2. **Evaluate each candidate sentence**:
   - Does this sentence directly support answering the question?
   - Does it align with or contradict the criteria in the question?
   - Is it sufficient on its own or only together with others?

3. **Select evidence sentences**:
   - Which sentence indices (0-based in the list above) should be retained as evidence?
   - You may select a subset of the candidates if not all are relevant.
   - If none are relevant, return an empty array [].

4. **Determine risk of bias**:
   - Based only on the evidence sentences you selected, apply the question criteria.
   - Decide: "low", "some concerns", or "high".
   - Justify in your reasoning field.

# Task
